Old Age Home

An "Old Age Home Project" is an initiative aimed at providing care, support, and a comfortable living environment for elderly individuals who may not have the means or the ability to live independently. The primary goal of such a project is to enhance the quality of life for senior citizens during their golden years, especially when they lack familial or societal support.
